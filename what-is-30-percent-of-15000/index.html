How to Calculate 30% of 15,000 — Step by Step

Calculating 30 percent of 15,000 is a common percentage problem. Whether you need this for shopping discounts, tax calculations, exam scores, or financial planning, here is the complete step-by-step solution.

The Formula

To find a percentage of a number, use this formula:

Result = (Percentage × Number) ÷ 100

1 Write the formula: Result = (30 × 15,000) ÷ 100
2 Multiply: 30 × 15,000 = 450000
3 Divide by 100: 450000 ÷ 100 = 4,500

Therefore, 30% of 15,000 is 4,500.

Alternative Method: Decimal Conversion

You can also convert the percentage to a decimal first:

  • Convert 30% to decimal: 30 ÷ 100 = .3
  • Multiply: .3 × 15,000 = 4,500
