How to Calculate 90% of 500 — Step by Step

Calculating 90 percent of 500 is a common percentage problem. Whether you need this for shopping discounts, tax calculations, exam scores, or financial planning, here is the complete step-by-step solution.

The Formula

To find a percentage of a number, use this formula:

Result = (Percentage × Number) ÷ 100

1 Write the formula: Result = (90 × 500) ÷ 100
2 Multiply: 90 × 500 = 45000
3 Divide by 100: 45000 ÷ 100 = 450

Therefore, 90% of 500 is 450.

Alternative Method: Decimal Conversion

You can also convert the percentage to a decimal first:

  • Convert 90% to decimal: 90 ÷ 100 = .9
  • Multiply: .9 × 500 = 450

Practical Uses

Knowing that 90% of 500 equals 450 is useful in many scenarios:

  • Shopping: 90% discount on an item priced at ₹500 saves you ₹450
  • Tax: 90% tax on ₹500 amounts to ₹450
  • Finance: 90% interest on ₹500 earns ₹450
