How to Calculate 25% of 2,500 — Step by Step

Calculating 25 percent of 2,500 is a common percentage problem. Whether you need this for shopping discounts, tax calculations, exam scores, or financial planning, here is the complete step-by-step solution.

The Formula

To find a percentage of a number, use this formula:

Result = (Percentage × Number) ÷ 100

1 Write the formula: Result = (25 × 2,500) ÷ 100
2 Multiply: 25 × 2,500 = 62500
3 Divide by 100: 62500 ÷ 100 = 625

Therefore, 25% of 2,500 is 625.

Alternative Method: Decimal Conversion

You can also convert the percentage to a decimal first:

  • Convert 25% to decimal: 25 ÷ 100 = .25
  • Multiply: .25 × 2,500 = 625
