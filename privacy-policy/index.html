Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

At Percentof ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect and use information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection when you use our website at percentof.in (the "Service").

Information We Collect

Our percentage calculator is designed to work without requiring any personal information from you. The numbers you enter into the calculator are processed entirely within your browser and are not transmitted to or stored on our servers. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information through the calculator functionality.

  • IP Address: Your IP address may be collected for analytics purposes and to help diagnose technical issues.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze site traffic, and understand user preferences.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Service Improvement: To understand how users interact with our calculator and identify areas for improvement.
  • Analytics: To analyze website traffic patterns and user behavior using services like Google Analytics.
  • Technical Maintenance: To diagnose and fix technical problems with our Service.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and legal obligations.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:
