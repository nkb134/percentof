How to Calculate 4% of 5,000 — Step by Step

Calculating 4 percent of 5,000 is a common percentage problem. Whether you need this for shopping discounts, tax calculations, exam scores, or financial planning, here is the complete step-by-step solution.

The Formula

To find a percentage of a number, use this formula:

Result = (Percentage × Number) ÷ 100

1 Write the formula: Result = (4 × 5,000) ÷ 100
2 Multiply: 4 × 5,000 = 20000
3 Divide by 100: 20000 ÷ 100 = 200

Therefore, 4% of 5,000 is 200.

Alternative Method: Decimal Conversion

You can also convert the percentage to a decimal first:

  • Convert 4% to decimal: 4 ÷ 100 = .04
  • Multiply: .04 × 5,000 = 200

Practical Uses

Knowing that 4% of 5,000 equals 200 is useful in many scenarios:

  • Shopping: 4% discount on an item priced at ₹5,000 saves you ₹200
  • Tax: 4% tax on ₹5,000 amounts to ₹200
  • Finance: 4% interest on ₹5,000 earns ₹200
  • Exams: Scoring 200 out of 5,000 marks equals 4%
