How to Calculate 50% of 500 — Step by Step

Calculating 50 percent of 500 is a common percentage problem. Whether you need this for shopping discounts, tax calculations, exam scores, or financial planning, here is the complete step-by-step solution.

The Formula

To find a percentage of a number, use this formula:

Result = (Percentage × Number) ÷ 100

1 Write the formula: Result = (50 × 500) ÷ 100
2 Multiply: 50 × 500 = 25000
3 Divide by 100: 25000 ÷ 100 = 250

Therefore, 50% of 500 is 250.

Alternative Method: Decimal Conversion

You can also convert the percentage to a decimal first:

  • Convert 50% to decimal: 50 ÷ 100 = .5
  • Multiply: .5 × 500 = 250

Practical Uses

Knowing that 50% of 500 equals 250 is useful in many scenarios:

  • Shopping: 50% discount on an item priced at ₹500 saves you ₹250
  • Tax: 50% tax on ₹500 amounts to ₹250
  • Finance: 50% interest on ₹500 earns ₹250
