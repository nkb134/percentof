Contact Us

We'd Love to Hear From You

At Percentof, we value feedback from our users. Whether you have a suggestion for improving our calculator, found a bug that needs fixing, have a question about how to use our tools, or simply want to share your experience, we're here to listen.

Your input helps us make Percentof better for everyone. Many of our improvements have come directly from user suggestions, and we're always looking for ways to enhance our service.

How to Reach Us

General Inquiries

For general questions, feedback, or suggestions about Percentof, please reach out to us via email. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days.

Email: contact@percentof.in

Technical Support

If you're experiencing technical issues with our calculator, such as incorrect calculations, display problems, or functionality errors, please include the following information in your message:

  • A description of the issue you encountered
  • The device and browser you were using
  • The specific calculation you were attempting
  • Screenshots, if possible

This information helps us diagnose and resolve issues more quickly.

Business Inquiries

For partnership opportunities, advertising inquiries, or other business-related matters, please contact us at the email address provided above with "Business Inquiry" in the subject line.

Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, you might find the answer to your question in the FAQ section on our calculator page. We've compiled answers to the most common questions about percentage calculations and how to use our tools.

Feature Requests

Have an idea for a new calculator or feature? We're always interested in expanding our offerings to better serve our users. When submitting a feature request, please describe:

  • What type of calculation or tool you'd like to see
  • How you would use this feature
  • Why you think it would be valuable to other users

While we can't guarantee that every suggestion will be implemented, we carefully consider all feedback when planning future updates.

Report an Issue

If you notice any errors in our calculations, broken links, or other problems with our website, please let us know. Accurate calculations are our top priority, and we take all error reports seriously. When reporting a calculation error, please provide:

  • The values you entered
  • The result you received
  • The result you expected
  • The specific calculator you were using

Response Time

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days. During peak periods, response times may be slightly longer. Rest assured that every message is read and will receive a response.

Stay Connected

We're continuously working to improve Percentof and add new features. Check back regularly to see what's new, or bookmark our calculator page for quick access to our tools.

Thank you for using Percentof. Your support and feedback make our service better every day.
