How to Calculate 10% of 400 — Step by Step

Calculating 10 percent of 400 is a common percentage problem. Whether you need this for shopping discounts, tax calculations, exam scores, or financial planning, here is the complete step-by-step solution.

The Formula

To find a percentage of a number, use this formula:

Result = (Percentage × Number) ÷ 100

1 Write the formula: Result = (10 × 400) ÷ 100
2 Multiply: 10 × 400 = 4000
3 Divide by 100: 4000 ÷ 100 = 40

Therefore, 10% of 400 is 40.

Alternative Method: Decimal Conversion

You can also convert the percentage to a decimal first:

  • Convert 10% to decimal: 10 ÷ 100 = .1
  • Multiply: .1 × 400 = 40

Practical Uses

Knowing that 10% of 400 equals 40 is useful in many scenarios:

  • Shopping: 10% discount on an item priced at ₹400 saves you ₹40
  • Tax: 10% tax on ₹400 amounts to ₹40
  • Finance: 10% interest on ₹400 earns ₹40
