How to Calculate 15% of 50,000 — Step by Step

Calculating 15 percent of 50,000 is a common percentage problem. Whether you need this for shopping discounts, tax calculations, exam scores, or financial planning, here is the complete step-by-step solution.

The Formula

To find a percentage of a number, use this formula:

Result = (Percentage × Number) ÷ 100

1 Write the formula: Result = (15 × 50,000) ÷ 100
2 Multiply: 15 × 50,000 = 750000
3 Divide by 100: 750000 ÷ 100 = 7,500

Therefore, 15% of 50,000 is 7,500.

Alternative Method: Decimal Conversion

You can also convert the percentage to a decimal first:

  • Convert 15% to decimal: 15 ÷ 100 = .15
  • Multiply: .15 × 50,000 = 7,500
